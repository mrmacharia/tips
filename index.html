Revenue Collection

Web Portal

Citizens are able to create unique profiles on the authority's official web portal. This portal provides a breakdown of all digitized services that citizens can pay online.

Mobile App

Citizens will download the government services payment app which will contain all digitized services. e.g business permits, parking, landrates & rents.

Hand-held devices

Our handheld devices will support collection of revenues e.g parking, bus parks, markets, ferry etc. They will also be able to issue receipts , licenses & permits.

Banks & agents

We will provide our platform to banks & agents for citizens to pay government fees through their channels e.g branches, mobile bankinking, internet banking etc.

RevenueSure

Main Functions

RevenueSure provides several functionalities and supports an authority to collect revenue, here we have the main funtions..

Billing.

This is an advanced billing platform that generates invoices for customers whenever they need to make a payment to the revenue authority. This enables the authority to track & report on all collections.

Collection.

The platform hosts channels of collection for both structured & unstructured revenue streams. Collection channels include mobile (App & USSD), web devices (terminals), & agency networks (banks).

Receipting.

The platform provides receipts for all payments received as proof of payment. Each type of payment will have its own customized receipt.

Customer Service & Issuance.

Each revenue stream will be supported with a service portal where customers can obtain certificates, licenses & permits.

Reporting.

The platform provides detailed dashboards of the report on collection & management activities. Collections are displayed on a real-time dashboard & data is presented in analytical format to show the status of revenue management for the authority.

Revenue Management

RevenueSure will enable the revenue authority to manage all revenue activities by;

Providing a single point of view for all services offered and payments received by the county. All payments will be automatically reconciled with government records where available.

Statistics Tracking

Providing analytics with insights on trends, patterns and provision of county services. This includes statistics on users and their requests.

Revenue enforcement

To ensure that all citizens pay for government services, we will implement several applications & methods to monitor, verify & ensure adherence to payments for services in the entire authority . These are;

  • Handheld devices; Our devices will enable enforcement officers to scan vehicle number plates, stickers & receipts to confirm parking fees were paid
  • Mapping of all revenue assets; We will tag all assets of the county using GIS & provide a map with details of who has paid & who has not paid.
  • Real-time dashboards and analysis; We will provide dashboards that will eneble specific revenue streams to follow up on due & overdue accounts
